Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

  • Analytics: to understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
  • Functionality: to ensure forms and sign-ups work correctly.
  • Marketing: to improve relevance of ads and offers.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work correctly.
