Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 2025

What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use our site. In addition to cookies, we may also use local storage and similar browser technologies to provide certain features.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
  • Functionality: To ensure forms and sign-ups work correctly. We also store your email locally after you sign up so our system can remember you across pages and deliver the free course and community experience without interruptions. This information stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.
  • Marketing: To improve the relevance of ads and offers.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies and clear local storage through your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work correctly if you do so. Clearing site data in your browser will remove any locally stored information such as your email.
